# 00-Vision-Exploration

## Purpose

This folder captures the earliest stage of ideation, discovery, and strategic thinking. Documents here represent exploratory work that helps shape the product vision before formal requirements are defined.

## What Goes Here

- **Brainstorming outputs**: Creative ideation sessions, concept exploration
- **Market research**: Industry analysis, market trends, opportunity identification
- **Innovation strategy**: Disruptive thinking, business model innovation
- **Feasibility studies**: Technical and business viability assessments
- **Initial concepts**: Early product ideas and hypotheses

## Document Types

| Type | Description | Example |
|------|-------------|---------|
| Brainstorming Output | Results from structured ideation sessions | `brainstorm-procurement-automation-2025-01.md` |
| Market Research | Industry and competitive landscape analysis | `market-analysis-procurement-saas-2025.md` |
| Opportunity Analysis | Strategic opportunity identification | `digital-transformation-opportunities.md` |
| Feasibility Study | Technical or business viability assessment | `blockchain-integration-feasibility.md` |

- `/bmad:bmm:workflows:research` (with research_type: market)

## Lifecycle

Documents in this folder are **exploratory and iterative**. They may be:
- Revisited as the project evolves
- Referenced when creating formal requirements
- Used to justify architectural decisions
- Archived if concepts are deprioritized
